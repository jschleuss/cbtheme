
	CRYSTAL BRIDGES COLLECTION

	- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

	Crystal Bridges Collection is a theme for displaying
	a large amount of art in a sortable, searchable
	format that uses custom fields to sort pieces. It is
	based on Elliot Jay Stocks's Starkers Theme for Wordpress.
